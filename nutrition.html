// setup (one-time)
  1. 1
    Open Telegram and message @BotFather. Send /newbot and follow the steps. You'll get a Bot Token like 7123456789:AAF...
  2. 2
    Start a chat with your new bot (click the link BotFather gives you). Send any message to it.
  3. 3
    Find your Chat ID: message @userinfobot — it replies with your numeric ID (e.g. 123456789).
  4. 4
    Paste both below and click Test Connection.
Your token is stored only in your browser's localStorage — it never leaves your device.
